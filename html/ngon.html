Description

NGon
A public display game that leverages the Oculus Rift and Kinect to create an asymmetric gameplay experience. The "Wizard" player grows, shrinks, and throws objects in an abstract platforming environment using Kinect gestures. The "Platformer" player can then jump on these platforms using an Xbox 360 controller and an Oculus Rift first-person. The goal is to collect keysthat free the Wizard from his castle prison.

Contribution Reel

My primary function on NGon was as Lead Programmer and Lead Designer. I developed game behaviors in response to Kinect gestures (Grow, Shrink, Throw objects) as well as general Oculus Rift player interaction (Move, Jump, Target objects, Respond to Kinect-influenced objects). I also mediated design discussions between the team and professor John Burton and made decisions on what features were possible in the 10 week lifecycle and would give players the most value.
