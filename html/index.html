About Me

I'm a graduate of the Drexel University Master's of Digital Media program. This alludes to my interests in videogame development, web design, animation, and the benefits technology can have on us.

Games

Stompster the Monster »Best described as a "Third-Person Upset Kaiju Simulator," Stompster tends to destroy his favorite city while trying to protect it. Save the city from alien invaders!

Avian »Avian is an educational game that uses birdwatching mechanics to teach basic bird identification. The game extends my graduate thesis on how player modeling can benefit from correlating player types and learning motivations through player metrics.

NGon »A public display game that leverages the Oculus Rift and Kinect to create an asymmetric gameplay experience. The "Wizard" player grows, shrinks, and throws objects in an abstract platforming environment using Kinect gestures. The "Platformer" player can then jump on these platforms using an Xbox 360 controller and an Oculus Rift first-person. The goal is to collect keysthat free the Wizard from his castle prison.

Animations

A Touch of Pink »Inspired by The Pink Panther cartoon series, this animation features Inspector ClouseauBot trying to find the pink culprit behind dastardly art vandalism.

Perfection »A lazy Perfection board game piece is pressured to act once an alarming timer starts ticking! Can he make it to his spot before time runs out?
